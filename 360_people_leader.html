  • 1.Base it on facts: Think of specific situations you've observed, not general impressions.
  • 2.Use 🚫 freely: If you haven't seen enough of a behavior to rate it, select "Not enough info" — it's the most honest answer.

  • 4.Avoid bias: Don't let one experience — positive or negative — influence all your other ratings.
